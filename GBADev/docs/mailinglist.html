The HAM Email List

How To Join
Want to share this great little thing you built using HAM? Feeling lonely with your bugs? Ask questions and join hundreds of other HAM coders on the HAM email list! Joining is easy:

  • send an empty email to hamdev-subscribe@yahoogroups.com
  • follow the instructions sent to you by Yahoo
How to view the list archives
Also easy. If you are a memeber, just visit http://groups.yahoo.com/group/hamdev
